About Me

My name is David Anderson. I am 27 years old and currently living in the Minneapolis area. For the past 6 years I have been employed in the healthcare field, managing group home facilities and over seeing small groups of staff to ensure day to day goals are met for clients. In my free time you can find me spending time with friends and family, writing music, playing videogames, or researching more info on coding!


A cool, fun fact about me is that I played drums in two touring bands, "Glass Houses" and "Zealot". Both bands were in the metal/hard rock genre of music. I was able to live the dream which was living in a van while traveling to new cities every night with some of my best friends.
